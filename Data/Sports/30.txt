
                                        MILLER--Edwin B.,("Big Ed" or "Eddie" to many), passed away on Wednesday, April 9, 2014 at the age of 82 at his home in Boca Raton, Florida. Ed was born on June 18, 1931 to Herman and Ruth Miller, and was raised in New Rochelle, NY
                                        with his sister Elaine. Following two years in the NBA with the Baltimore Bullets, Ed had a successful career in the toy business for over 40 years, culminating in the establishment of his own partnership, Miller-Sperber,
                                        Inc. Ed is survived by his loving wife of 44 years, Helaine Miller along with their son Richard and his sister Elaine Newman. Friends and family will best remember Ed for his sharp wit, sense of humor, and charmingly
                                        gregarious nature.
