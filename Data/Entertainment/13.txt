AdvertisementJULY 26, 1853
The steamship Northern Light, Capt. J. MINER, arrived at this port yesterday from San Juan de Nicaragua, whence she sailed on the 17th inst. She left New-York on the 5th of July at 3 P. M., and arrived at San Juan on the 15th, at 11 A. M., stopping at Kingston for coal, which place she left at 5 P. M. on the 12th.
