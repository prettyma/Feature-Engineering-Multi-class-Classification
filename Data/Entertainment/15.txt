AdvertisementJUNE 3, 1866
From the Albany Argus. The Directors of the New-York, Oswego and Midland Road are exerting themselves to secure the necessary assent of the towns on the route to issue bonds in aid of the work. Meetings are being held in the different villages and towns, and it is reported that the feeling in favor of granting aid is almost universal.
