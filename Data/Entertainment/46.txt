AdvertisementNOV. 11, 1856
The United States Mail Steamship Ballic, Capt. COMSTOCK, arrival at this port yesterday, from Liverpool, Oct. 29, bringing news four days later. A complimentary correspondence passed between the Ballic's passengers and Capt. COMSTOCK on the passage out. The Africa arrive at Liverpool on the 27th.
