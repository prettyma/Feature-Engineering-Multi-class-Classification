AdvertisementDEC. 30, 1886
WASHINGTON, Dec. 29.--All the preparations for the removal of the body of Senator Logan from the home in which he died to the Capitol have now been completed, and to-night Calumet-place presents a more quiet appearance than at any time since the Senator died.
