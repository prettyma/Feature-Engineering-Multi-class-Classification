AdvertisementDEC. 3, 1910
Declines were again the rule yesterday in the Curb market, and few issues failed to show substantial losses at the close. Chino copper continued its downward course, with a loss of 5/3 on the day's trading. The price opened at 23 1/2, declined to 22 1/8, and closed after transactions of 7,200 at 22 5/8.
