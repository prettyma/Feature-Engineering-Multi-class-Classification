AdvertisementFEB. 29, 1884
Hubert O. Thompson, Commissioner of Public Works, yesterday transmitted to the Mayor his report of the transactions of his department for the quarter and year ending Dec. 31, 1883. The total expenditure for the year was $4,714,276 13, a decrease of $324,231 91 from the previous year.
