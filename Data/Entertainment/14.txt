AdvertisementSEPT. 17, 1867
Messrs. BONNEY, STEWART and CONNOLLY were present at the session of the Board yesterday. The following were the principal claims submitted: STEPHEN O'ROURKE, $70, for back pay of sweeps of Clinton Market. It appears that the Common Council raised the pay of sweeps from $50 to $60 per month, and claimant failed to receive the extra $10 per month.
