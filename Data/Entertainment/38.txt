AdvertisementNOV. 27, 1868
The only news of moment, out of the masses of unimportant and conflicting intelligence that come to us from a dozen sources, relates to THE SITUATION AT PUERTO PRINCIPE. It is rumored that the Spanish garrison at that place is besieged by a force of insurgents tour times as numerous as they, and that owing to the scarcity of provisions their capitulation is looked for.
